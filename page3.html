A Preliminary Overseas Market Evaluation: Brazil

Investigated the viability of Operating Local Irish children's Toy manufacturer Lottie Dolls internationally. This was done by establishing characteristics of an ideal market for Lottie Dolls to enter into, determining the ideal overseas market, and matching the firm's capabilities with the opportunities in the foreign market within Brazil.
