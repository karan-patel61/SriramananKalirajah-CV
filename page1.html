ECO-Ride – Smarter Travel Campus

Established a marketing campaign revolved around a new shuttle service at the Institute of Technology Carlow. ECO-Ride is focused on targeting IT Carlow students that live within a 3 km radius of the campus area. The purpose of this campaign is to change behavior and limit the amount of students who drive to campus. By educating students on how to be more environmentally-friendly and reduce their carbon footprint​!
